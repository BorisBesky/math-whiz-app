📐 Geometry (4.G)

📍 Points, Lines, Rays, and Angles

The building blocks of geometry! Every shape is made from these basic elements.

🔵 Point

•

An exact location with no size

📏 Line

↔️

Goes on forever in both directions

➡️ Ray

→

Starts at a point and goes on forever in one direction

📐 Angle

∠

Formed when two rays meet at a point

🎯Memory Tip: A ray is like a flashlight beam - it starts somewhere and keeps going!

🔺 Classifying Triangles

Triangles can be classified by their sides and by their angles!

By Sides:

Equilateral
All 3 sides equal
🔺
Isosceles
2 sides equal
🔺
Scalene
All sides different
🔺

By Angles:

Right Triangle
Has one 90° angle
📐
Acute Triangle
All angles less than 90°
🔺
Obtuse Triangle
One angle greater than 90°
🔺

🔷 Classifying Quadrilaterals

Four-sided shapes have special names based on their properties!

🟦 Square
• All sides equal
• All angles are 90°
• Opposite sides parallel
📱 Rectangle
• Opposite sides equal
• All angles are 90°
• Opposite sides parallel
🔶 Rhombus
• All sides equal
• Opposite angles equal
• Opposite sides parallel
🔧 Parallelogram
• Opposite sides equal
• Opposite angles equal
• Opposite sides parallel
🪂 Trapezoid
• Exactly one pair of parallel sides
• Can have different shapes
🏠 Quadrilateral
• Any four-sided shape
• General term for all 4-sided figures
Fun Fact: A square is a special type of rectangle, and a rectangle is a special type of parallelogram!

↔️ Line Symmetry

A shape has line symmetry if you can fold it along a line and both halves match perfectly!

Examples of Symmetrical Shapes:

Butterfly 🦋
1 line of symmetry
(vertical through middle)
Square ⬜
4 lines of symmetry
(2 diagonal, 2 through sides)
Circle ⭕
Infinite lines of symmetry
(any line through center)
🪞Symmetry Test: Imagine folding the shape along a line. If both sides match exactly, it has line symmetry!

📏 Angle Measurement

Angles tell us how much something turns. We measure them in degrees!

Acute Angle
Less than 90°
🔺
Right Angle
Exactly 90°
📐
Obtuse Angle
Between 90° and 180°
📐
Straight Angle
Exactly 180°
↔️
Real Life Angles:
• Corner of a book = 90° (right angle)
• Open scissors = acute angle
• Laptop half-open = obtuse angle
• Flat table = 180° (straight angle)
