Step 2: The Slow Way - Addition üêå

You could add them up the long way:
3 + 3 + 3 + 3 = 12
That works, but it's slow!
What if you had 100 plates with 3 cookies each? You'd have to write 3 one hundred times and add them all up!

Step 3: The Fast Way - Multiplication! ‚ö°

Multiplication is a shortcut! When you have the same number repeated multiple times, you can multiply instead of adding.
4 √ó 3 = 12
This means "4 groups of 3" or "3 taken 4 times". The √ó symbol means "times" or "groups of".
Much faster!
Instead of writing 3 + 3 + 3 + 3, we just write 4 √ó 3. Same answer (12), but way quicker!

Remember: The first number tells you how many groups you have, and the second number tells you how many items are in each group.
