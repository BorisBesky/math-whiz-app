Different-looking fractions can show the same amount!
Two fractions are equivalent if they represent the same part of a whole, even if the numbers look different. For example, 1/2 and 2/4 are the same amount because if you cut a pizza into 2 slices and eat 1, it's the same as cutting it into 4 slices and eating 2.

To find an equivalent fraction, multiply the top and bottom by the same number. To simplify back, divide the top and bottom by the same number.
